Who are we?

Team Schnaps is more than just a community, we are enthusiasts, and friends united by our passion for bouldering. Founded back in 2020 and located in Berlin. We believe that bouldering is for everyone. Our diverse and inclusive team has members of various backgrounds, ages, and abilities.

Team Schnaps is not just about climbing, but about having fun and having a good time. Beyond the gym walls, we foster a sense of community that extends into everyday life. We often make gatherings to communicate and share experiences, with a glass of a tipsy drink.

How to join us?

We are not in an active search for new members. But if you are friendly and cool person you can try your luck by reaching us. Success is not guaranteed.
